About

Prof. Michael Good is an Associate Professor in the Department of Physics at Nazarbayev University. Prof. Good received the B.Sc. in Physics with Highest Honors from the Georgia Institute of Technology, under the advisement of Prof. David R. Finkelstein. Good then attended the University of Hawaii as a Manoa Scholar in the Physics and Astronomy Department and the Institute of Astronomy. Good obtained the M.Sc. and PhD. in Physics from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He was advised by both Prof. Charles R. Evans and Prof. Paul R. Anderson. As a PhD. student, Good was a visiting researcher at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics at Cambridge University in 2009. From 2011 to 2014 he was a Postdoctoral Scientist and Senior Research Fellow in Singapore at the Institute of Advanced Studies at the Nanyang Technological University in the quantum cosmology research group.
